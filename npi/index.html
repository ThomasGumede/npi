  • University / College / School

    ‐ St Patricks Private Hostel, located in Libode, is just a 2-minute walk (110m) from St Patricks High School, providing students with convenient access to their school.
    ‐ Nowethu House, situated in Mthatha's Ikhwezi Township, is close to several educational institutions, including a 6-minute walk (400m) to Zimele High School, a 4-minute walk (290m) to Ngangelizwe High School, and a 16-minute walk (1.1km) to Nozuko Secondary School. The hostel offers easy access to these schools, making it an ideal accommodation choice for students in the area.

  • Study Rooms

    Both properties provide a conducive environment for academic success with onsite Study Hubs. Nowethu House also offers a combined Study Hub and TV room, ensuring a versatile space for both study and relaxation.

  • Shopping

    For shopping needs, Nowethu House residents are just a 22-minute walk or 4-minute drive (1.9km) from Khwezi Spar. Additionally, the Engen Filling Station and BTNgebs are both 5 minutes by car or 22-26 minutes on foot (2.1km).

  • Other

    For extracurricular activities and recreation, residents of Nowethu House can access the Rotary Stadium within a 13-minute walk (1.0km). The Ikhwezi Public Library, just a 2-minute walk (130m) from Nowethu House, provides additional study resources and quiet spaces for learning.

St Patricks Private Hostel

Zitatele Administration Area, Gxulu Location, Libode, 5160

Description

St Patricks Private Hostel is a mixed-gender student residence located in the serene Zitatele Administration Area, Gxulu Location, Libode. Just a 2-minute walk (110m) from St Patricks High School, this hostel offers a range of amenities, including uncapped WiFi, backup power, limited backup water, 24-hour security, CCTV, a study hub, laundry room, and secure individual lockers. With 14 shared rooms and an onsite Student Life Manager, it provides a safe and comfortable living environment tailored to students' needs.

St Patricks Private Hostel, located in Libode, is just a 2-minute walk (110m) from St Patricks High School, providing students with convenient access to their school.
